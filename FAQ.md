# DJO Dictee FAQ
### Where do I find it?
The project can be found on [dictee.djoleden.nl](https://dictee.djoleden.nl).

### How do I participate?
- Just ask ngkon;
- Make a lot of spelling mistakes when ngkon is nearby, he will invite you right away.

### How does it work?
This is clearly explained on the homepage, please take a look over there :)

### How many correct answers do I need to pass?
At least 50% (even amount of answers) or a little more than 50% (odd amount of answers).  
The rules, shown to you when trying to join a dictee, happily explain the details for you.

### Are there any rewards?
Sometimes. If at least two people participated in a dictee, the examiner can decide
to award a prize to the particpiant with the most correct answers.
